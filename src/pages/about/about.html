Nosotros

The Power and Energy Society (PES) Chapter of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) Ecuador Section is the host of the 2017 IEEE PES Conference on Innovative Smart Grid Technologies Latin America (ISGT-LA 2017) that takes place from September 19 to September 23, 2017 in Quito, Ecuador.

The Conference is a forum for the participants to discuss “Opportunities and Challenges of the Power Industry towards Management Systems Integration”. The aim of the Conference is to join researchers and practitioners from industry and academy and to provide them with a platform to report on recent developments, achievements, deployments, technology trends and research results, as well as initiatives related to Smart Grids. The Conference features plenary sessions, panels, technical papers, industry exhibitions, workshops, tutorials by experts on smart grid applications, and technical visits. The main addressed are current issues arising from the operation and control of smart grids, such as smart management systems, grid integration of renewable energy resources, power system stability, demand response and management, energy efficiency, storage devices, smart metering, smart mobility, smart building, smart cities, information technologies (IT), signal processing, intelligent control, among others.

The interest of IEEE PES Ecuador Chapter is to provide scientific and engineering information on electric power and energy for the betterment of the Ecuadorian and Latin American society, and to offer a preferred professional development source for our members. Therefore, the organization of the IEEE PES ISGT-LA 2017 has the aim of not only sharing the smart grid state-of-the-art worldwide advances to the Ecuadorian scientific community, but also to show the Ecuador’s most remarkable smart grid developments in Generation, Transmission and Distribution to the world scientific community. Among these developments are the construction of significant power infrastructure (e.g. large Hydroelectric Power Plants and a 500-kV Transmission Line) and the implementation of state-of-the-art technology for the intelligent monitoring and control of both transmission and distribution systems, (e.g. a Wide Area Monitoring System WAMS, a Special Protection Scheme for ensuring the system integrity SPS, and a whole set of sophisticated hardware and software that makes up an Integrated Distribution Management System SIGDE).


The Organizing Committee welcomes you to Quito, and hopes you will enjoy all the activities prepared as part of the IEEE PES ISGT-LA 2017.

STEERING COMMITTEE CHAIR

El Instituto Nacional de Eficiencia Energética y Energías Renovables (INER), cumpliendo con sus funciones, creó, planificó, organizó y ejecutó en 2013 el primer Congreso Internacional en Sostenibilidad Energética, que tuvo una segunda edición en el 2015. Estos eventos generaron interés en el público académico y en investigadores, por lo que se asumió el compromiso de realizar este evento cada dos años. El Congreso I+D+i en Sostenibilidad Energética, ha ganado prestigio y empieza a ser considerado un evento referencial en la región, por lo que se ha procurado garantizar su continuidad. Esperamos que en esta oportunidad usted pueda sacar el mayor provecho de nuestras ponencias magistrales, eventos especializados y exposición de artículos académicos y posters.
